About Us

Empowering Street Vendors, Strengthening Supply Chains

At Market Mitra, we are on a mission to digitally empower India’s street vendors by simplifying how they access raw materials.

For decades, vendors have faced challenges like:

  • High dependence on middlemen
  • Inconsistent supply and fluctuating prices
  • Lack of access to verified suppliers

We aim to change this by building a simple and trustworthy platform where vendors can directly connect with raw material suppliers, compare prices, and place orders — all from their phones.

Our Vision

To bridge the gap between vendors and suppliers, ensuring better margins for vendors and consistent business for suppliers, while uplifting India’s grassroots economy.

Why Us?

  • Built for vyapaari by understanding their needs
  • Local language support and easy-to-use tools
  • Affordable access to suppliers without middlemen

"Bridging vendors and suppliers for a stronger tomorrow."

"Empowering grassroots businesses with the right connections."
